Explore The Beauty of France

France has been the worlds most popular tourist destination for over 25 years. Let’s explore.

Famous Places in the France.

Eiffel Tower

Originally constructed as the entrance to the 1889 World’s Fair to celebrate the 100th anniversary of the French Revolution, today it is what people think of first when they think of France. Measuring 321 metres tall, the equivalent to 81 stories, the Eiffel tower serves not only as a national monument and major tourist attraction but also as an observation and radio broadcast tower.

The Disneyland Paris

Since 1992, Disneyland Paris, originally called Euro Disney resort has been drawing crowds from all over the world. Currently it is the number one tourist attraction in all of France and Europe, even beating out the Eiffel Tower and the Louvre. It is also the 16th most popular tourist attraction in the world. 14.8 million people visited Disney Paris in 2015 according to their annual report.

The Louvre and art

The Louvre which we know today is not only one of the largest museums in the world, housing over 460,000 pieces of art and artifacts.Initially built as a fortress in the late 12th century, it was converted to the the main residence for French kings in the 16th century. Then in 1682 the Louvre primarily become a place to display the royal collection.100 years later during the French revolution, the National Assembly decreed that the Louvre should be used as a museum to display the nation’s masterpieces.

The Versailles Palace

Every year over 3 million people travel to Versailles to see how former French royalty lived. Everywhere you look is an amazing delight. It’s embellished by generations of lavish gardes, landscape, architecture, sculptures, decorations, art and more! Some of the more popular things to see at the palace include the State Apartments, the incredible Hall of Mirrors, the Versailles Gardens and The Trianons.

The French Alps

Alps, stretching 1,200 km across eight Alpine countries including France’s French Alps.French Alps which attract roughly 60-80 million visitors each year.Lot's of Activities available in Alps.Those are skiing, snowboarding, mountaineering, biking and rock climbing to name a few.

Other Famous Things

The French train transportation

The Gare du Nord railway station in the heart of Paris France. With over 29,000 km of railway, visitors to France can easily travel from one end of France to the other quickly without the need for a car.The public transport system in and around Paris is probably the best of any city in Europe in terms of geographical spread, speed, upkeep and tariffs.

Events & Festivals

In France there are more than 20 festivals and events conducted every year. They all celebrate French history and myth with spectacular festivals. There’s no better time to learn the smaller idiosyncrasies of local French culture than during a colorful festival. This also one of the important reason for tourist attraction.

French Foods

French food is also a major draw for tourists. The French mealtime tradition,with all its wine pairing, social rituals, the setting of a beautiful table and associated skills and crafts that the French are renowned for was even granted UNESCO World Heritage Status in 2010 when it was added to the representative list of “Intangible Cultural Heritage of Humanity”.
